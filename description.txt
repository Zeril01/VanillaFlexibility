This mod currently adds:

* 3 fishing poles
* 2 flails
* 3 spears
* 6 bullets
* 10 consumables for ranged

Tips on how to find some items

* Bone Ball can be bought from a Skeleton Merchant
* Endless Rainbow Pouch falls from the Empress of Light
* Endless Wasp Pouch is always found in the Dungeon's Jungle Chest

Compatible with any mod
